Conora Table

This prototype project was created as a challenge to create a dynamic bar chart which updates and stores new data via LocalStorage. Updating the total opens a dialog in which a new total and date may be entered. Click the "Reset" button in the dialog (on the first run) or supply your own data. The table is hard-coded to 700k but dynamic bounds are possible. Hopefully, the Covid virus won't make that necessary.
