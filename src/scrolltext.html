ScrollText

Singing in a band, I had dozens of pages to flip through for song lyrics. I wrote ScrollText, which lets me combine songs into a single file and automatically generate a TOC. Speed, delay, color, and font size are adjustable by large buttons. Comments can be added. Text markup is required for advanced features. It's also useful for public speaking scripts.
